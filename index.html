Wings of fire

by Arun Tiwari

Every common man who by his sheer girt and hard work
achieves success should share his story with the rest for
they may find inspiration and strength to go on, in his
story. The 'Wings of Fire' is one such autobiography by
visionay scientist Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, who from very
humble beginnings rose to be the President of India. The
book is full of insights, personal moments and life
experiences of Dr. Kalam. It gives us an understanding on
his journey of success.

The 3 mistakes of my life

by Chetan Bhagat

The 3 Mistakes of my life is the third novel written by
eminent Inidian Author Chetan Bhagat. Based on cricket,
business and religion, the novel is set against the
backdrop of beautiful city Ahmedabad. Revolving around
three young Indian boys Omi, Ishaan and Govind, the
book goes on to narrate how the three are trying their
best to make ends meet in the city. Based on real events,
the books starts with a dramatic twist, where Bhagat is
reading an e-mail sent by some young person Govind.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter's life is miserable. His parents are dead and

about himself he's wizard. A mysterious visitor rescures

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. After a
